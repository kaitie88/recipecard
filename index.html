Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

My favorite comfort food is 100% chocolate chip banana bread. No matter the day or the weather, I always love this dish. It's an easy to make recipe, that anyone can do. Also, it doesn't have to be the same every time! Sometimes I make blueberry banana bread, other times I make chocolate chip walnut banana bread. It's always changing depending on my mood, which I find very conveinient! Although it's my comfort food, it is still pretty healthy. It's nice being able to eat something that tastes like a desert, but will not affect my health like one.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter(room temperature)
  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 3-4 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour or all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips plus extra

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl or stand mixer, cream together the butter and brown sugar for about 2 minutes until well combined.
  3. Add the egg then add the mashed bananas. Mix for 30 seconds, scraping down the sides of the bowl. Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and vanilla. Mix everything until all is well combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Place a folded piece of parchment paper in a 9x5 loaf pan then spray the pan with non-stick spray. This will help remove the loaf later.
  5. Pour the mixture into the loaf pan. Bake for 60-80 minutes until a knife can be inserted in the center of the loaf and it comes out clean. Let cool for at least 30 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
